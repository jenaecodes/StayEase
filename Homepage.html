🧭 Interactive Prototype Instructions

  1. Welcome to StayEase: Scroll to view the Homepage in its entirety, then click the arrow to the right of the “check-out” date to begin your search for Mallorca, Spain (2 adults, 10/06/25–10/28/25).
  2. Scroll to view the Search Results Page. Click the “Explore Map View” button in the top Nav bar.
  3. Scroll through the Map Page, then click “Back to Search” above the map.
  4. Scroll down the Search Results Page to find “Grand Beach Resort” and click “View Details.”
  5. On the Hotel Details Page, view the page fully. Try the “Back to Search” button. Then click “Book Now” for the “Deluxe King Suite.”
  6. Scroll through the Room Details Page. Try the “Back to Hotel Details” button. Then click either “Book Now” button on the page.
  7. On the Add-ons Page, view all options. Try “Back to Room Details,” then click “Add to Total.”
  8. Scroll through the Payments Page. Try “Back to Add-ons,” then click “Confirm Booking.”
  9. Choose to sign-up or continue as a guest. Click “Continue.”
  10. Congratulations! Your booking is confirmed! Scroll through the Confirmation Page. You can click “Back to Home” or the “StayEase” logo.
