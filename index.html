Jenny's Hotel Booking Experience

🧭 Interactive Prototype Instructions

  1. Welcome to StayEase: First, click "Got it" to view my prototype. Scroll to view the Homepage in its entirety, then please click on the arrow to the right of the “check-out” date to begin your search for your destination of Mallorca, Spain (2 adults, 10/06/25–10/28/25).
  2. Scroll to view the Search Results Page in its entirety. In the top Nav bar, click the “Explore Map View” button to view the map page.
  3. Scroll to view the Map Page in its entirety. Then, click the “Back to Search” button above the map to return to the search results.
  4. On the Search Results Page, scroll down to find the hotel called, “Grand Beach Resort”, then click the “View Details” button.”
  5. Scroll to view the Hotel Details Page in its entirety. Feel free to try the “Back to Search” button, then navigate back to the hotel destination details. On the Hotel Details Page, find the “Deluxe King Suite” and click on the “Book Now” button.
  6. Scroll to view the Room Details page in its entirety. Feel free to try the “Back to Hotel Details” button, then navigate back to the room details. Then, click on the “Book Now” button either below the paragraph of text describing the room, or the “Book Now” button at the bottom of the page.
  7. Scroll to view the Add-ons Page in its entirety. Feel free to try the “Back to Room Details” button, then navigate back to the add-on details. Click on the “Add to Total” button.
  8. Welcome to the Payments Page, please scroll to view this page in its entirety. Feel free to try the “Back to Add-ons” button, then navigate back to the payment details. Click on the “Confirm Booking” button.
  9. Here it will ask you to either sign-up or continue as a guest. Please click the “Continue” button to continue as a guest.
  10. Congratulations! Your hotel booking at the Grand Beach Resort in Mallorca, Spain has been confirmed! Please scroll through this page in its entirety. Feel free to click the “Back to Home” button, or the “StayEase” logo in the navigation bar.

Thank you for testing out my Hotel Booking Website Prototype for Desktop!
